Our DevSecOps Approach

DevSecOps recognizes that security is not a phase but an integral part of the entire software development lifecycle. Our DevSecOps services bring together development, security, and operations teams to collaborate from the outset, enabling a proactive and holistic approach to security.

Key Features of EraCloud DevSecOps Services

  • Security by Design: Infuse security into your development processes from the initial design phase. Our experts work closely with your teams to embed security best practices into the architecture and development workflows.
  • Automated Security Testing: Implement automated security testing throughout the development pipeline. Identify and address vulnerabilities early in the process to minimize security risks and reduce the cost of remediation.
  • Continuous Compliance: Ensure compliance with industry regulations and security standards. Our DevSecOps services include continuous monitoring and validation to keep your systems in line with the latest security requirements.
  • Incident Response and Recovery: Develop robust incident response and recovery plans. In the event of a security incident, our DevSecOps practices enable swift identification, containment, eradication, and recovery.
  • Collaborative Security Culture: Foster a culture of collaboration between development, security, and operations teams. Our DevSecOps practices prioritize communication and knowledge-sharing, ensuring that security is everyone's responsibility.
